# People First Bank

> People First Bank (trading name of Heritage and People's Choice Ltd, ABN 11 087 651 125) is an Australian customer-owned mutual bank formed from the 2023 merger of Heritage Bank and People's Choice Credit Union. Under Australia's Consumer Data Right (CDR / open banking) it exposes a public, unauthenticated Product Reference Data (PRD) API conforming to the Data Standards Body Consumer Data Standards. Consumer data sharing beyond PRD runs through the accredited-data-recipient model (OAuth2/OIDC, FAPI) via the CDR register, not a general-purpose public developer platform.

## APIs

- [People First Bank CDR Product Reference Data API](https://www.peoplefirstbank.com.au/help-and-support/open-banking/open-banking-for-developers): Public, unauthenticated CDR PRD API. Base: https://public.openbanking.peoplefirstbank.com.au/cds-au/v1 — GET /banking/products (Get Products, x-v 5) and GET /banking/products/{productId} (Get Product Detail, x-v 7).
